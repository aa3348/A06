Andrew Ross Sorkin

Andrew Ross Sorkin is a renowned financial journalist, author, and television personality. He is best known for his work as the founder and editor-at-large of DealBook, a financial news service published by The New York Times. Sorkin has written several best-selling books on finance and economics, and he is a frequent guest on financial news programs, where he provides expert analysis and commentary. His insightful perspectives on the world of finance make him a sought-after speaker at conferences and events worldwide.
